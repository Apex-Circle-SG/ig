The Science of NAD+ and Cellular Energy

To understand the hype surrounding this nationwide rollout, one must understand the biology of NAD+. This coenzyme is a key player in mitochondrial function, the powerhouses of our cells. Mitochondria rely on NAD+ to facilitate the electron transport chain, the process by which our bodies convert food into ATP (adenosine triphosphate), the primary energy currency of the cell. When NAD+ levels are high, cellular energy production is optimized, leading to improved physical stamina, mental clarity, and metabolic efficiency.
