Kokology Quiz: What the Form of Your Soul Reveals About Your True Self

2025-08-28

Quiz Question

Imagine your soul survives after death.
👉 What form do you think it would take?

  1. Same size and shape of your body
  2. Bigger in size
  3. Become tiny like a fairy
  4. Become a flame or cloud, without definite form

Answer Guide & Reasoning

1. Same size and shape of your body

  • Answer: You accept yourself for who you are.
  • Reasoning: Choosing this form reflects a sense of inner peace and contentment with your identity.

2. Bigger in size

  • Answer: You still have many goals left to achieve.
  • Reasoning: This shows ambition, a desire for growth, and the belief that your potential extends far beyond your current self.

3. Become tiny like a fairy

  • Answer: You are great, but haven’t yet discovered your best qualities.
  • Reasoning: This choice suggests hidden strengths and a self-image that underestimates your true potential.

4. Become a flame or cloud, without definite form

  • Answer: You don’t concern yourself with weaknesses or strengths.
  • Reasoning: This reflects a free spirit, unconstrained by limitations or labels, flowing with life as it comes.
