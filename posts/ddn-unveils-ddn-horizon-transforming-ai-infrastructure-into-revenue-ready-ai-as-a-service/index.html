What is DDN Horizon?

DDN Horizon is an integrated software platform designed to sit atop the high-performance storage environments DDN is known for, providing a unified control plane for AI infrastructure. By centralizing management, DDN Horizon allows organizations to orchestrate resources effectively, ensuring maximum utilization of GPUs and high-speed data paths. Whether you are a cloud service provider or a large enterprise, the platform offers the agility required to scale services dynamically.

Key Architectural Benefits

  • Multi-Tenancy Support: Securely partition compute and storage resources for different business units or external customers, enabling a true provider model.
  • Automated Resource Allocation: Through intelligent policy management, systems can automatically assign data throughput based on the priority of the AI training or inference workload.
  • Seamless Integration: Built to complement DDN’s industry-leading NVMe storage solutions, ensuring that data pipelines never starve for performance.

Addressing the Data Bottleneck in GenAI

Training generative AI models is a data-hungry enterprise. If the data is not delivered to the GPU fast enough, the entire system stalls, leading to spiraling operational costs. DDN Horizon is built with the understanding that data is the lifeblood of AI. It leverages advanced parallel file systems to ensure that data delivery is never the bottleneck in a multi-user, multi-tenant environment.

Optimizing Data Pipelines

The success of an AI platform relies on how efficiently it can move unstructured data. DDN Horizon provides:
