FAQ

Do AI trading bots guarantee profits?

No, there is no such thing as a guaranteed profit in the stock market. AI bots are tools that help manage risk and execute strategies, but they are still subject to market volatility and technical failures.

Are these bots legal for retail investors?

Yes, using automated trading software is completely legal. Most reputable platforms adhere to strict SEC and financial authority regulations regarding trade execution and data privacy.

How much capital do I need to start?

Many modern AI services are designed for accessibility and allow users to start with as little as $100, though larger portfolios often allow for more diversified automated strategies.

Can I lose money with an AI bot?

Absolutely. If the underlying market strategy is flawed or if the market experiences extreme, unpredictable events, the bot may generate losses. Always monitor your bot’s performance periodically.

Which bot is best for beginners?
