Kokology “Blue Coat, Yellow Coat” Quiz: Discover Who Brings You Joy and Who Turns Cold

2025-08-25

Quiz Question

On a cold Christmas night, you decide to watch the lightings with a group. One person is wearing a blue coat, another is wearing a yellow coat.
Who are these people you know?

Answer Guide & Reasoning

  1. Answer: Blue coat is someone who gives you the cold shoulder
    • Reasoning: The blue coat symbolizes distance and indifference. This person represents someone in your life who may ignore, reject, or show little warmth toward you.
  2. Answer: Yellow coat is someone you feel happy about
    • Reasoning: The yellow coat reflects warmth, energy, and joy. This person symbolizes someone whose presence brings you positivity, comfort, and happiness.
