The global threat landscape has changed dramatically over the last year. From ransomware attacks targeting healthcare systems to supply chain breaches affecting global logistics, the sophistication of these threats is increasing. At the expo, attendees will dive deep into the mechanics of these attacks and, more importantly, how to build resilience against them.

Key Focus Areas for 2025

  • AI-Driven Defense Mechanisms: Exploring how artificial intelligence and machine learning can be leveraged to detect threats in real-time.
  • Cloud Security Compliance: Navigating the complex regulatory requirements of data sovereignty and cloud infrastructure protection.
  • Zero Trust Architecture: Implementing security models that assume breach and continuously verify every access request.
  • Human-Centric Security: Addressing the insider threat and the critical need for comprehensive cybersecurity awareness training for employees.
