We Tried the Pattern Blow Dryer—Here Are Our Honest Thoughts

2026-05-10

Introduction to the Pattern Blow Dryer

When Tracee Ellis Ross launched Pattern Beauty, it quickly became a staple for those with curly, coily, and tight-textured hair. The brand has always focused on celebrating and nurturing natural hair patterns, so when they announced their entry into the world of styling tools with the Pattern Blow Dryer, the beauty community took notice. We decided to put this high-tech tool to the test to determine if it truly delivers on its promise of efficient, heat-safe styling for textured hair types.

Design and Ergonomics

The first thing you notice when unboxing the Pattern Blow Dryer is its sleek, ergonomic design. Unlike traditional bulky salon dryers, this device feels lightweight and well-balanced. This is crucial for anyone with thick hair who knows the struggle of arm fatigue during a long wash-day session. The buttons are intuitive, and the soft-touch finish provides a premium feel that resists slipping during use.

The Attachments

A blow dryer is only as good as its attachments, especially when dealing with various curl patterns. The Pattern Blow Dryer comes with a suite of accessories designed to meet specific needs:

  • The Diffuser: A wide-diameter bowl that is perfect for encouraging natural curl definition without creating frizz.
  • The Concentrator Nozzle: Ideal for precision styling and smoothing the hair cuticle.
  • The Wide-Tooth Comb: A game-changer for detangling and stretching coils from root to tip.
  • The Brush Attachment: Great for achieving a blowout look while maintaining volume.

Performance and Heat Settings

The core of the Pattern Blow Dryer experience is its performance. It features multiple heat and speed settings, allowing for a customized approach depending on your hair’s porosity and current state. The ionizing technology is built-in to help reduce frizz and static, which is a common pain point for curly-haired individuals. We found that the heat distribution is remarkably even, preventing hot spots that often lead to heat damage.

Is It Really Faster?

One of the biggest claims made by the brand is the reduction in drying time. Our testing confirmed that the airflow is powerful enough to significantly cut down the time spent under the dryer. By using the wide-tooth comb attachment, we were able to stretch and dry sections of hair in half the time it would take with a standard consumer-grade dryer. This efficiency is a major benefit for those who view wash day as a time-consuming chore.

The Verdict: Is It Worth the Price?

Investing in a high-end hair tool is a significant decision. The Pattern Blow Dryer sits at a premium price point, but when you consider the quality of the materials and the intentional design for textured hair, it begins to make sense. It is clear that this tool was engineered with the specific challenges of curly hair in mind, such as the need for gentle heat and specialized attachments that don’t snag or break delicate strands.

If you wear your hair in a variety of styles—from wash-and-go curls to sleek, stretched blowouts—the versatility of this tool offers great value. It effectively replaces the need for multiple styling devices, saving you space and money in the long run.

Tips for Best Results

To get the most out of your Pattern Blow Dryer, we recommend following these steps:

  • Always use a heat protectant: Regardless of the tool, protecting your strands from high temperatures is essential for long-term health.
  • Start with damp hair: Do not use the dryer on soaking wet hair; towel-dry or air-dry slightly to minimize heat exposure.
  • Section your hair: Working in small, manageable sections ensures even drying and prevents tangling.
  • Use the cool shot button: Finish your style with a blast of cool air to lock in shine and set your curls or blowout in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Pattern Blow Dryer safe for color-treated hair?

Yes, the dryer features adjustable heat settings that allow you to use lower temperatures, which is critical for maintaining the integrity of color-treated hair and preventing fading.

Does the dryer come with a warranty?

Pattern Beauty offers a limited warranty on their styling tools. It is recommended to register your product on their official website immediately after purchase to ensure you are covered.

Can I use the attachments on other blow dryers?

The attachments are specifically engineered to fit the Pattern Blow Dryer nozzle. They are not universal and likely will not fit other brands securely.

How do I clean the air intake filter?

The rear filter is removable and should be cleaned regularly to ensure optimal airflow and prevent the motor from overheating. Simply twist it off and wipe away any dust or hair accumulation with a dry cloth.

Is this tool suitable for fine, curly hair?

Absolutely. Because the dryer offers precise control over heat and speed, those with finer textures can set the dryer to a lower intensity to avoid over-drying or damaging their hair, while still benefiting from the ionic technology.

Final Thoughts

The Pattern Blow Dryer is more than just a piece of hardware; it is a thoughtful tool that respects the diversity of curly and coily hair textures. While it is a premium investment, the combination of speed, ergonomic comfort, and hair-health-focused features makes it a standout choice for anyone serious about their hair care routine. If you are tired of struggling with tools that weren’t designed for your hair type, this device is certainly worth a spot on your vanity.
