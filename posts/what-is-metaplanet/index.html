What is Metaplanet?

2025-05-09

Metaplanet Inc. (TSE: 3350.T) is a Tokyo-based public company that has transformed itself into Japan’s first dedicated Bitcoin Treasury Company, applying its core cash flows and strategic capital to accumulate Bitcoin as its principal reserve asset. Founded in 1999 with diversified operations spanning hotel management, real-estate and Web3 consulting, Metaplanet reorganized in 2024 to deploy excess cash into Bitcoin purchases, targeting 10,000 BTC by the end of 2025 and 21,000 BTC by 2026. As of May 2025, the company holds 5,555 BTC—valued at over $570 million—under an aggressive accumulation strategy funded through zero-interest bonds and equity issuances. Metaplanet’s pivot has garnered inclusion in the CoinShares Blockchain Global Equity Index and positioned it as a model for corporate Bitcoin adoption in Asia.

Company Background

Metaplanet was incorporated in 1999 and originally focused on hotel operations, blockchain consulting, and digital-asset ventures in Japan. For over a decade, it engaged in real-estate asset renovation underpinned by emerging blockchain technologies before undergoing a pandemic-driven restructuring in 2021 and 2022. In early 2024, under CEO Simon M. Gerovich—a former Goldman Sachs derivatives trader—the company formally adopted a Bitcoin-first treasury policy, leveraging BTC’s apolitical monetary policy and scarcity as a strategic hedge.

Bitcoin Treasury Strategy

Metaplanet views Bitcoin as its principal treasury reserve asset, directing excess cash flow into systematic BTC accumulation. Between March and May 2025, the company disclosed multiple Bitcoin acquisitions—including 150 BTC on March 24, 2025, 696 BTC on April 1, 2025, and 555 BTC on May 6, 2025—bringing its total holdings to 5,555 BTC. To finance purchases, Metaplanet has issued zero-interest, convertible bonds and utilized equity offerings, mirroring strategies pioneered by U.S. peers like Marathon Digital and MicroStrategy.

Financial Position & Market Recognition

Trading under ticker 3350.T on the Tokyo Stock Exchange, Metaplanet maintains a market capitalization of approximately ¥239 billion (US$1.6 billion) and an enterprise value near ¥234 billion. The firm’s aggressive Bitcoin holdings—representing nearly 35% of its market cap—have earned it a slot in the CoinShares Blockchain Global Equity Index, signifying its status among leading blockchain-oriented public companies.

Services and Expertise

Beyond serving as a corporate Bitcoin treasury, Metaplanet provides consulting services to enterprises seeking to integrate Bitcoin into their balance sheets and operations. Leveraging its cross-sector expertise in finance, trading, real estate, and digital assets, the company offers strategic guidance on corporate reorganizations, treasury management, and blockchain product development.

Leadership and Governance

Metaplanet’s leadership team combines seasoned professionals with backgrounds in global finance and blockchain. CEO Simon M. Gerovich drives the Bitcoin strategy, while COO Yoshimi Abe oversees operations and community engagement in Japan. The board’s commitment to transparent reporting—publishing detailed BTC acquisition schedules—sets a governance benchmark in the corporate crypto space.

Future Outlook

With ambitious targets of 10,000 BTC by end-2025 and 21,000 BTC by 2026, Metaplanet is poised to become one of Asia’s largest corporate Bitcoin holders. Continued issuance of strategic financing instruments and potential partnerships in Bitcoin infrastructure could further solidify its role as a pioneering model for digital-asset treasury management in public markets.
