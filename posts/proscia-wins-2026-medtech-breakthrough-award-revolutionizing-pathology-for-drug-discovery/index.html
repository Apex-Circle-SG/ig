Frequently Asked Questions

What is the MedTech Breakthrough Award?

The MedTech Breakthrough Award is an annual program that recognizes the most innovative companies, products, and services in the global health and medical technology market. Winning this award signifies excellence in technological innovation and tangible impact within the healthcare sector.

How does Proscia accelerate drug discovery?

Proscia accelerates drug discovery by digitizing pathology workflows and applying AI-powered analytics to tissue samples. This allows researchers to rapidly identify disease patterns, screen potential drug candidates more efficiently, and optimize patient selection for clinical trials, thereby shortening the research cycle.

Is Proscia’s technology limited to cancer research?

While Proscia has made significant strides in oncology due to the complexity of tumor analysis, their digital pathology and AI platform is highly versatile. It can be applied to a wide range of therapeutic areas where tissue analysis is a critical component of research and diagnosis, including immunology and neurology.

How does digital pathology differ from traditional pathology?

Traditional pathology relies on the physical examination of glass slides under a microscope. Digital pathology involves scanning these slides into high-resolution digital images, which can then be analyzed, stored, and shared using software. This digital format enables the use of AI to perform complex, quantitative analysis that is impossible with a microscope alone.

What is the significance of the ‘Moment of Diagnosis’?

The ‘moment of diagnosis’ refers to the critical point when a patient’s tissue sample is analyzed to determine the presence and nature of a disease. Proscia believes this moment is a goldmine of data. By capturing this data digitally, they unlock insights that help pharmaceutical companies better understand disease progression and develop more effective, targeted therapies.
