6 of the Best Metaverse ETFs to Buy in 2023: Navigating the Future of Digital Assets

2026-05-18

Understanding the Metaverse Investment Opportunity

The metaverse represents the next evolution of human interaction and digital economy. As companies pour billions into virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and blockchain connectivity, investors are seeking efficient ways to capture this growth. Instead of picking individual winners in a volatile nascent market, Metaverse ETFs provide a diversified basket of stocks poised to benefit from the burgeoning digital ecosystem.

What are Metaverse ETFs?

An Exchange-Traded Fund (ETF) is a security that tracks an index, sector, commodity, or other asset. A metaverse ETF specifically aggregates firms involved in the development of virtual worlds, gaming engines, specialized hardware, and cloud infrastructure required to support the metaverse. These ETFs offer retail and institutional investors a risk-mitigated pathway into high-growth tech sectors.

Top 6 Metaverse ETFs for 2023

1. Roundhill Ball Metaverse ETF (METV)

As the first ETF specifically designed to track the performance of the metaverse, METV remains the industry benchmark. It focuses on companies actively involved in the seven layers of the metaverse, including computing, networking, and virtual platforms. Its largest holdings historically include giants like NVIDIA, Microsoft, and Roblox.

2. ProShares Metaverse ETF (VERS)

VERS tracks the Solactive Metaverse Theme Index. It is meticulously structured to include companies that provide the essential building blocks of the metaverse. This includes firms focused on digital entertainment, social platforms, and the specialized hardware needed to facilitate immersive 3D experiences.

3. Fidelity Metaverse ETF (FMET)

Fidelity has entered the space with FMET, offering a lower expense ratio compared to some competitors. This fund targets companies that develop, manufacture, distribute, or sell products related to the metaverse. It is an excellent choice for long-term investors looking for institutional-grade management in the frontier tech space.

4. Global X Metaverse ETF (VR)

The Global X Metaverse ETF focuses heavily on the hardware infrastructure side of the coin. Because the metaverse requires massive processing power and specialized optical hardware, this fund captures the companies that build the literal foundation for a digital world, such as semiconductor manufacturers and VR headset developers.

5. Evolve Metaverse ETF (MESH)

For those looking for international exposure, the MESH ticker offers a broad mandate. It includes global equities that are positioning themselves for a future where commerce and social life occur predominately in digital environments. Its geographic diversification helps mitigate region-specific regulatory risks.

6. Franklin Metaverse UCITS ETF (METU)

Designed for European investors or those looking for UCITS-compliant instruments, METU maintains a strict focus on companies with high-quality balance sheets that are investing heavily in research and development for Web3 and immersive internet technologies.

The Risks and Rewards of Investing in the Metaverse

While the potential for growth is immense, investors must acknowledge the volatility inherent in frontier technology. Regulatory uncertainty, privacy concerns, and the actual time frame for mainstream metaverse adoption are significant factors. Investors should treat these ETFs as long-term holdings rather than short-term trading vehicles. The goal is to gain exposure to companies that will define the digital standards of the next decade.

Evaluating Key Metrics for Metaverse ETFs

When selecting your preferred ETF, consider the expense ratio, assets under management (AUM), and the thematic overlap of the portfolio. High AUM usually indicates higher liquidity, making it easier to buy and sell shares during market turbulence. Additionally, look at the fund’s top ten holdings to ensure they align with your personal risk tolerance regarding tech-heavy portfolios.

FAQ Section

What is the benefit of buying a Metaverse ETF over individual stocks?

Investing in a single company carries the risk that your chosen business might fail to innovate. An ETF provides instant diversification, protecting you from the failure of one specific company while still capturing the growth of the overall metaverse industry.

Are Metaverse ETFs safe for long-term investing?

Metaverse ETFs are considered speculative growth investments. They are suitable for investors with a longer time horizon who can withstand market volatility in exchange for potential high-growth exposure to emerging tech.

How do I start investing in these ETFs?

You can purchase these ETFs through any standard brokerage account. Simply search for the ticker symbol (such as METV or VERS), research the current prospectus, and execute the trade as you would with any other stock or ETF.

Is 2023 a good time to buy into the Metaverse?

Many experts argue that recent market corrections have provided more attractive entry points for metaverse-focused funds. As the technology matures, the valuation of these companies becomes more anchored to real-world infrastructure and software revenue rather than pure hype.
