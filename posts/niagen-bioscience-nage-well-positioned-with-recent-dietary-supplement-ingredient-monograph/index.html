Understanding the Role of NAD+ in Human Health

To grasp why the NAGE monograph is so vital, one must first understand the biological importance of NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide). This coenzyme is found in every living cell and is essential for energy metabolism, DNA repair, and gene expression. As we age, natural levels of NAD+ decline, contributing to various age-related health challenges. Niagen Bioscience has dedicated years of research to perfecting efficient precursors that help restore these levels, providing a biological boost to cellular vitality.
