The term “Ideal Cut” is often used loosely in the jewelry industry, but for Ginza Diamond Shiraishi, it is a strict scientific standard. Their design team utilizes advanced optical modeling to determine the precise angles and proportions required for each individual stone. This ensures that light entering the diamond is reflected internally and returned through the top of the stone, creating that coveted fire and brilliance. In their Hong Kong flagship, customers can observe how slight variations in the pavilion angle or table percentage can drastically alter the visual performance of a diamond engagement ring.

The Art of Polishing and Setting

The artisans in the Hong Kong workshop employ techniques such as pave setting, prong setting, and bezel setting with extreme precision.
