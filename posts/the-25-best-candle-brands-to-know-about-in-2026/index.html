FAQ

How should I properly care for my candles?

Always trim your wick to 1/4 inch before each burn to prevent soot and ensure an even melt pool. Allow the wax to melt all the way to the edges of the vessel during the first burn to tunnel prevention.

What wax is best for a clean burn?

Soy wax, coconut wax, and beeswax are generally considered the cleanest burning options. They are renewable, biodegradable, and produce significantly less soot than traditional paraffin wax.

How do I maximize the scent throw of my candle?

Place your candle in a central location in the room away from drafts or air conditioners. For larger rooms, consider using two matching candles on opposite sides of the space for a more balanced scent experience.
