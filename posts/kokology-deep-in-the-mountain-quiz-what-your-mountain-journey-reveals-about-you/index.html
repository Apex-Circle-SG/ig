Kokology “Deep in the Mountain” Quiz: What Your Mountain Journey Reveals About You

2025-08-25

Quiz Questions

You journey to a mountain. Answer the following:

  1. You search for a rare stone. What is your impression of the mountain?
  2. The sun has set and you haven’t found the stone. What will you do next?
  3. You finally find the stone. What is its size, weight, and value?
  4. What parting words will you say to the mountain?

Answer Guide & Reasoning

  1. Answer: What is the impression of your dad
    • Reasoning: Your initial impression of the mountain reflects your subconscious view of your father. A majestic or intimidating mountain may indicate respect or awe, while a calm or approachable mountain shows warmth and closeness.
  2. Answer: How do you deal with life situations
    • Reasoning: Your actions when the sun sets reveal your coping style. Persisting shows determination, giving up reflects resignation, and adapting indicates flexibility in life’s challenges.
  3. Answer: What is your self-worth
    • Reasoning: The size, weight, and value of the stone represent how you perceive your own abilities and value. A large, heavy, valuable stone reflects confidence, while a smaller or less valuable stone may suggest humility or self-doubt.
  4. Answer: What you always wanted to say to your dad
    • Reasoning: Your parting words to the mountain symbolize unspoken thoughts or emotions toward your father, representing admiration, gratitude, or unresolved feelings.
