Kokology Quiz: Where the Fish Hits Reveals What You Protect Most

2025-08-26

Quiz Question

You went fishing, and as the fish reels out of the water so fast, it suddenly hits you.
Where on your body did the fish hit?

Answer Guide & Reasoning

Your chosen body part

  • Answer: That part of your body is what you most want to protect
  • Reasoning: The instinctive spot you imagine being hit reveals your subconscious sense of vulnerability. It highlights areas—physical or emotional—that you safeguard the most.
