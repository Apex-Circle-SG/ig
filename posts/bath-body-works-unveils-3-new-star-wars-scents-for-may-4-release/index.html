The Force Awakens: Exploring the Three New Scents

Bath & Body Works has meticulously crafted three distinct fragrance profiles, each paying homage to the themes and aesthetics of the Star Wars universe. Here is a breakdown of what you can expect when these products hit the shelves.

1. Dark Side: The Power of Obsidian and Spice

For those who prefer the mysterious and bold, the Dark Side candle offers a complex scent profile. It features notes of dark cocoa, black amber, and a hint of spicy clove. This fragrance is designed to evoke the intensity of the Galactic Empire, providing a sophisticated and deep aroma that fills any room with an air of authority and mystery.

2. Light Side: A Galactic Oasis of Serenity

Contrasting the intensity of the Dark Side, the Light Side scent is a breath of fresh air. With notes of white jasmine, crisp ozone, and soft sandalwood, this fragrance captures the essence of a tranquil planet like Naboo or the peaceful meditation of a Jedi Master. It is clean, uplifting, and perfect for creating a relaxing atmosphere in your living space.

3. The Force: An Ethereal Blend

The centerpiece of the collection is undoubtedly The Force. This scent is an enigmatic blend of stardust, moonflower, and sparkling citrus. It represents the balance between the light and the dark, offering a scent that is both grounded and otherworldly. It is intended to capture the magic of the cinematic experience itself.
