Strategic Shifts in Cyber Defense

The opening keynote emphasized a shift from reactive defense to proactive threat hunting. This paradigm shift requires a fundamental change in how security teams operate. Instead of waiting for an alert, teams are encouraged to assume a state of persistent compromise and hunt for indicators of malicious activity within their environments.

  • Zero Trust Implementation: Moving away from perimeter-based security to identity-centric models.
  • Supply Chain Security: Tightening controls over third-party software and vendors.
  • Automated Incident Response: Utilizing SOAR platforms to minimize the mean time to respond (MTTR).
  • Human-Centric Security: Investing in employee training to mitigate the risk of social engineering.
