Top Observability Tools for 2025

2025-05-06

As artificial intelligence (AI) systems become increasingly integral to various industries, ensuring their reliability, performance, and transparency is paramount. Observability tools play a crucial role in monitoring, debugging, and optimizing AI applications, particularly those involving large language models (LLMs) and conversational agents. This guide introduces a curated list of leading AI observability tools that are shaping the future of AI deployment and maintenance.

1. Cekura (formerly Vocera)

Cekura specializes in testing and monitoring voice AI agents. It allows developers to simulate thousands of conversational scenarios using real audio, workflows, and personas. Key features include parallel calling, actionable evaluations, and real-time insights with detailed logs and trend analysis. Cekura’s intuitive dashboard and instant alerting system ensure that AI voice agents perform optimally across various scenarios.

2. Arize AI

Arize AI offers a unified observability and evaluation platform for AI applications. Its Arize AX platform accelerates the development and perfection of AI apps and agents in production. Features include instant visibility with seamless instrumentation, automated evaluations, real-time monitoring with anomaly detection, and customizable metrics. Arize AI is deployed by thousands of AI teams, making it a trusted choice for comprehensive AI observability.

3. Coval

Coval provides a simulation and evaluation platform for AI voice and chat agents. It enables developers to simulate conversations using scenario prompts, transcripts, workflows, or audio inputs. Coval’s platform offers built-in and custom metrics for evaluating agent performance, real-time monitoring of production calls, and instant alerts for performance thresholds. Its foundation in autonomous testing ensures robust and reliable AI agent deployment.

4. Langfuse

Langfuse is an open-source LLM engineering platform that assists in monitoring, tracing, and debugging AI agents. It offers features like LLM tracing, prompt management, evaluation, and metrics to improve LLM applications. Langfuse integrates with various tools such as LangGraph, Llama Agents, Dify, Flowise, and Langflow, providing a comprehensive solution for AI observability.

5. Foundry.ai

Foundry.ai is a technology studio that creates AI software products and companies in partnership with global enterprises. While not a traditional observability tool, Foundry.ai focuses on building practical AI applications that drive measurable business performance improvements. Their approach emphasizes the importance of observability in deploying AI solutions that deliver immediate and tangible profit impacts.

6. Helicone

Helicone offers an all-in-one platform to monitor, debug, and improve production-ready LLM applications. It provides features such as dashboards, request tracking, session segmentation, prompt management, and experiment evaluation. Helicone integrates with various AI providers, including OpenAI, Anthropic, and Azure, ensuring seamless observability across different platforms.

7. Comet

Comet provides an end-to-end model evaluation platform for AI developers. It offers tools for LLM evaluations, experiment tracking, model production monitoring, and artifact management. Comet’s Opik LLM Evaluation allows developers to run automated evaluations on LLM responses, ensuring optimal application performance before and after deployment. Its comprehensive suite of tools makes it a valuable asset for AI observability and lifecycle management.

Conclusion

The observability tools highlighted in this guide are instrumental in ensuring the reliability, performance, and transparency of AI applications. By leveraging these platforms, developers and organizations can monitor, debug, and optimize their AI systems effectively, leading to enhanced user experiences and operational efficiencies.
