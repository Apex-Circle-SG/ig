We Tried Pattern Beauty’s Styling Products for a Full Month: An Honest Review

2026-05-09

Introduction: The Hype Behind Pattern Beauty

When Tracee Ellis Ross launched Pattern Beauty, the natural hair community took notice. Designed specifically for curly, coiled, and tight-textured hair, the brand promised moisture, definition, and a celebration of natural hair patterns. After months of seeing the iconic amber bottles all over social media, we decided it was time to put the styling range to the ultimate test. We committed to a full month of using only Pattern Beauty styling products to see if they could truly handle the demands of diverse hair textures.

The Testing Methodology

For this 30-day trial, we focused on three core styling products: the Curl Gel, the Styling Cream, and the Edge Control. Our tester has Type 4C hair that is prone to dryness and shrinkage. We wanted to evaluate the products based on three critical metrics: moisture retention, hold strength, and product buildup. Throughout the month, we avoided all other brands to ensure that our results were strictly attributed to the Pattern Beauty formulas.

Week 1: First Impressions and Application

The first application was a revelation. The Styling Cream has a rich, decadent texture that immediately softened the hair strands. Unlike many other products that leave a waxy residue, the cream absorbed well. We paired it with the Curl Gel for a wash-and-go style. The gel provided a medium-flexible hold that didn’t leave the hair feeling crunchy or stiff. By the end of the first week, the definition was clear, and the hair felt significantly more hydrated than it did with our previous routine.

Week 2: Managing Moisture and Hydration

One of the biggest struggles for textured hair is maintaining moisture throughout the week. By day four of our second week, we noticed that the hair didn’t feel brittle. The Pattern Beauty Styling Cream acts as an excellent sealant. We experimented with a braid-out style, and the results were impressive. The hair retained its shape and bounce, and the typical frizz that usually appears by mid-week was noticeably absent. It is clear that the brand prioritizes hydration above all else.

Week 3: Testing the Edge Control

No styling routine is complete without a reliable edge control. The Pattern Beauty Edge Control was tested during a high-humidity day. We found that it provided a strong hold without the dreaded white flakes. It smoothed the edges perfectly and lasted through an entire workday. The consistency is thick, which is a major plus for those with coarser hair textures, yet it remains easy to manipulate with a small brush or a toothbrush.

Week 4: Long-Term Results and Product Buildup

By the end of the month, the primary concern was whether these products would cause heavy buildup. Because Pattern Beauty products are formulated without harsh sulfates, the buildup was minimal compared to other brands. The hair remained soft and manageable. We did notice that a little goes a long way; using too much product can weigh down fine-textured hair. However, for those with thick, dense curls, the generous application is exactly what is needed for lasting definition.

The Verdict: Is Pattern Beauty Worth It?

After 30 days, our consensus is that Pattern Beauty delivers on its promises. It is a high-quality line that respects the integrity of textured hair. While the price point is slightly higher than drugstore alternatives, the performance, ingredient quality, and the overall experience of using the products justify the investment. If you are struggling with dryness or are looking for a reliable, consistent styling routine, this range is a fantastic choice.

Pros of Pattern Beauty Styling Products

  • Deep Hydration: The products excel at keeping hair moisturized for days.
  • Ingredient Integrity: Formulated with high-quality ingredients that nourish rather than strip the hair.
  • Versatility: Works well for both wash-and-go styles and protective styles like braids or twists.
  • Non-Flaky Hold: The gel and edge control offer hold without the white residue common in cheaper brands.

Cons to Consider

  • Price Point: Higher than standard mass-market products.
  • Product Weight: Can be heavy if over-applied on looser curl patterns.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pattern Beauty safe for color-treated hair?

Yes, Pattern Beauty products are sulfate-free and formulated to be gentle on all hair types, including color-treated hair. They focus on moisture, which is essential for maintaining the health of hair that has been chemically processed.

Does the Curl Gel leave a crunchy finish?

Not at all. The Curl Gel is designed to provide hold while maintaining the natural softness of the curl. Once the hair is fully dry, you can gently ‘scrunch out the crunch’ to achieve a soft, touchable finish.

How long does the Styling Cream last?

A little goes a long way. Depending on your hair density and length, a single jar of the Styling Cream can last anywhere from six weeks to three months. It is very concentrated, so we recommend starting with a small amount and adding more only if needed.

Can I use these products on fine hair?

You certainly can, but you should be mindful of the quantity. Start with a dime-sized amount of the Styling Cream to avoid weighing down your hair. The lighter products in the line might be better suited for fine hair if you find the cream too heavy.

Are the products vegan and cruelty-free?

Pattern Beauty is committed to being a cruelty-free brand. They prioritize clean ingredients and do not test on animals, making them a great choice for conscious consumers.

Final Thoughts

Our month-long journey with Pattern Beauty was a resounding success. The products helped us achieve defined, healthy-looking curls without the hassle of constant re-application. Whether you are a beginner in the natural hair world or a seasoned pro, these products offer a reliable foundation for your styling needs. We recommend starting with the Styling Cream and Curl Gel duo to see the difference for yourself.
