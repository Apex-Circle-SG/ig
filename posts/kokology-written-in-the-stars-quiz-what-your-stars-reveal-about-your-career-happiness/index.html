Kokology “Written in the Stars” Quiz: What Your Stars Reveal About Your Career Happiness

2025-08-25

Quiz Questions

You are stargazing.

  1. Draw three stars of different sizes.
  2. Turn one of the stars into a comet.

Answer Guide & Reasoning

  1. Answer: Biggest star represents your career
    • Reasoning: The largest star shows how you view your job. If all three stars are around the same size, it suggests dissatisfaction or lack of excitement in your work. If one star is significantly larger, it means you feel stable and fairly content with your career path.
  2. Answer: If the comet is your biggest star, it is time to change job
    • Reasoning: Transforming the largest star into a comet symbolizes fleeting energy and burnout. This reflects that your career may no longer be fulfilling, and it could be a sign that you are ready to explore new opportunities or make a bold change.
