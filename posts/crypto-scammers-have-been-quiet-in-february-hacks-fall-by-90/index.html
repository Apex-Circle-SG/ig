Understanding the Decline in Crypto Scams and Hacks

Another significant factor is the implementation of enhanced security measures by cryptocurrency exchanges and platforms. These measures have made it more challenging for scammers and hackers to infiltrate systems and steal funds.

Increased regulatory pressure has also played a role in curbing crypto scams and hacks. Governments and regulatory bodies worldwide have been tightening their grip on the cryptocurrency market, implementing stricter regulations and compliance requirements. This has made it more difficult for bad actors to operate within the space.
