Kokology Quiz: What Does Your Volcano Reaction Reveal About How You Handle Anger?

2025-08-26

Quiz Questions

You are watching an erupting volcano from a safe distance. What is your first thought?

  1. “Maybe this is nature’s way of warning us to stop destroying the earth”
  2. “I guess the pressure just got too high inside there”
  3. “Wow – what a sight! I’m impressed”
  4. “It’s in the nature of these things to blow up. Big deal”

Answer Guide & Reasoning

1. “Maybe this is nature’s way of warning us to stop destroying the earth”

  • Answer: You thought you are responsible for people’s anger.
  • Reasoning: You tend to personalize outbursts, believing that others’ frustrations may be connected to your own actions or words.

2. “I guess the pressure just got too high inside there”

  • Answer: You often blame others when things go wrong.
  • Reasoning: Rather than reflecting inward, you assume external pressure or someone else’s fault is the cause of the problem.

3. “Wow – what a sight! I’m impressed”

  • Answer: You accept criticism and outbursts in a positive way.
  • Reasoning: Instead of feeling attacked, you recognize emotional explosions as natural expressions and handle them with composure.

4. “It’s in the nature of these things to blow up. Big deal”

  • Answer: You can say anything out and do not pay attention to it.
  • Reasoning: You’re unfazed by drama and quick tempers, often brushing them off as trivial rather than taking them to heart.
