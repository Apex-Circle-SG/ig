The Iconic Candle Designers Swear By Just Got Its First Makeover in 63 Years

2026-05-01

A Legacy Redefined: The Candle That Changed Interior Design

For over six decades, one specific candle silhouette has served as the silent protagonist in the world of high-end interior design. Often spotted in the background of architectural digest spreads and sitting atop the marble mantels of world-renowned designers, this candle is more than just a source of light—it is a design object. After 63 years of unwavering consistency, the brand has finally unveiled its first major makeover, sending ripples of excitement through the design community.

The History of an Icon

To understand the magnitude of this redesign, one must first look at the history of the object. Launched in the early 1960s, the candle was designed with a specific philosophy: form follows function, but elegance dictates the atmosphere. Its minimalist shape, distinct wax blend, and signature scent profile became the gold standard for luxury homes. Designers have long sworn by it because it manages to bridge the gap between contemporary minimalism and timeless traditionalism.

Why Designers Choose This Specific Candle

Designers are notoriously picky about the details that populate their projects. This candle has remained a staple for several key reasons:

  • Timeless Aesthetic: The silhouette fits seamlessly into any room, whether it is a mid-century modern living room or a classic Parisian apartment.
  • Scent Complexity: Unlike mass-market candles, this brand utilizes master perfumers to create complex, layered scents that do not overwhelm the senses.
  • Burn Quality: The proprietary wax blend ensures a clean, even burn that prevents tunneling, a common issue with cheaper alternatives.
  • Sustainability: In recent years, the brand has doubled down on ethically sourced ingredients, making it an easy choice for eco-conscious clients.

Inside the 63-Year Makeover: What Has Changed?

When a brand with such a rich history decides to change, the stakes are incredibly high. The goal was not to reinvent the wheel, but to refine it for a new generation. The updates are subtle, focused on sustainability and modern manufacturing techniques, while preserving the soul of the original design.

Refined Vessel Design

The most immediate change is the vessel. While the iconic shape remains, the glass has been updated to be thinner yet more durable, using recycled materials. This change not only reduces the carbon footprint of production but also allows for better light diffusion, creating a warmer glow when the candle is lit.

The New Wax Formulation

The brand has introduced a new vegetable-based wax blend that burns 20 percent longer than the previous version. This is a game-changer for luxury consumers who expect longevity alongside performance. The scent throw is also more consistent, thanks to an improved wick centering process that ensures the candle burns perfectly from the first light to the last.

Packaging and Sustainability

In response to modern environmental standards, the packaging has undergone a complete overhaul. The brand has moved away from plastic-heavy inserts to fully compostable, FSC-certified cardboard. This commitment to the planet is something today’s consumers demand, and the brand has delivered without sacrificing the unboxing experience.

How to Style Your New Iconic Candle

With its fresh look, the candle is even more versatile than before. Here is how top interior designers recommend styling it in your home:

  • The Entryway Statement: Place the candle on a console table alongside a stack of art books and a sculptural bowl. The soft glow welcomes guests immediately.
  • Bathroom Sanctuary: Create a spa-like atmosphere by placing the candle near a bathtub. The new vessel design catches the light beautifully against tile surfaces.
  • Coffee Table Centerpiece: Group the candle with other objects of varying heights, such as a brass object or a small vase, to create a curated vignette.

The Future of Luxury Home Fragrance

This makeover signals a broader trend in the home decor industry. Brands are realizing that heritage is important, but adaptability is essential. By honoring the 63-year legacy while embracing modern sustainability, this brand has ensured its place in the homes of the next generation of design lovers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the scent of the candle different after the redesign?

No, the brand has kept the original, beloved scent formulations exactly the same. They understand that the fragrance is the core of the product’s identity, so they focused their efforts on improving the vessel and the burn quality rather than changing the olfactory experience.

Will the new candles fit into existing holders and accessories?

Yes, the dimensions of the candle have remained consistent with the historical sizing. This ensures that anyone who owns existing candle accessories, cloches, or decorative holders can continue to use them with the updated versions.

Is the new wax better for the air quality in my home?

Absolutely. The new proprietary wax blend is cleaner-burning than the original, resulting in significantly less soot emission. This makes it a safer and healthier choice for indoor environments, especially in smaller rooms or spaces with limited ventilation.

Where can I purchase the redesigned version?

The updated candles are currently available through the brand’s official website, as well as select luxury home decor boutiques and high-end department stores. Due to the high demand following the announcement, some retailers may have limited stock.

Does the price reflect the new changes?

While there has been a slight adjustment to reflect the higher costs of sustainable manufacturing and premium materials, the brand has maintained a price point that keeps it within the reach of their core luxury demographic. The increased burn time also provides better value for money over the long term.

Final Thoughts

The decision to update an icon is always a risk, but in this case, it is a triumph. By focusing on quality, sustainability, and subtle design improvements, the brand has successfully bridged the gap between its storied past and a more conscious future. Whether you are a long-time fan of the brand or a newcomer looking to invest in a piece of design history, this updated candle is a must-have addition to your home.
