Kokology “On Your Birthday” Quiz: Discover Who Truly Matters in Your Life

2025-08-25

Quiz Question

Who sends you a birthday card that you never expected?
Who gives you the biggest birthday present?

Answer Guide & Reasoning

  1. Answer: Someone you worship will send you a card
    • Reasoning: The unexpected card represents people you admire or hold in high regard. It shows who genuinely acknowledges and values you, even if you didn’t anticipate it.
  2. Answer: Someone with the biggest present is the one you often take for granted
    • Reasoning: The person giving the largest gift symbolizes overlooked support in your life. It reflects those whose presence, efforts, or care you may not fully appreciate.
