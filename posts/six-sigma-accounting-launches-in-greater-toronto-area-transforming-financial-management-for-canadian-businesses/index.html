The Six Sigma Advantage: DMAIC in Finance

The power of our service lies in the DMAIC methodology. Here is how we apply it to your business:

  • Define: We identify your specific financial goals and the critical-to-quality (CTQ) characteristics of your accounting processes.
  • Measure: We collect current data to establish a baseline of performance.
  • Analyze: We identify the root causes of financial discrepancies or inefficiencies.
  • Improve: We implement targeted solutions to streamline your operations.
  • Control: We establish monitoring systems to sustain improvements over the long term.
