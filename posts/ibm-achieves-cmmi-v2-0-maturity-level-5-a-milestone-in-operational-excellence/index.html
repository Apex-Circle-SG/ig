What is CMMI V2.0?

The CMMI V2.0 (Capability Maturity Model Integration) is a world-class performance improvement framework. It provides businesses with the essential elements of effective processes, helping them improve their performance, key capabilities, and business results. Unlike previous versions, V2.0 is designed to be more accessible, easier to understand, and faster to implement, while remaining highly rigorous.

At the Maturity Level 5 stage, an organization is considered to be in a state of Optimizing. This means that the organization is not just performing tasks correctly, but is actively using quantitative analysis to understand process variations and drive innovation. This level of maturity ensures that risks are managed proactively and that the organization can consistently deliver high-quality outcomes even under challenging conditions.
