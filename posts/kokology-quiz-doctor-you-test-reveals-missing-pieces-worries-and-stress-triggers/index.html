Kokology Quiz: Doctor You Test Reveals Missing Pieces, Worries, and Stress Triggers

2025-08-28

Quiz Questions

Imagine you are a psychotherapist with your own private counseling practice.

  1. Describe the room in detail where you hold your therapy sessions.
  2. What does your first patient want to talk to you about?
  3. How does your patient react when you give your professional advice?
  4. You are doing paperwork when suddenly someone barges in. Who is this person?

What do your answers reveal about your hidden self?

Answer Guide & Reasoning

1. The Therapy Room

  • Answer: What you describe as missing or present in the room = something you feel missing from your life.
  • Reasoning: The setting you create reflects what your subconscious believes you lack or deeply desire in your own world.

2. The First Patient’s Topic

  • Answer: The patient’s problem = your own worries.
  • Reasoning: The first concern that comes to mind often mirrors what occupies your own hidden anxieties or preoccupations.

3. The Patient’s Reaction

  • Answer: Their reaction = how you accept good advice when it’s given to you.
  • Reasoning: The way your patient responds is a projection of your own ability (or inability) to take constructive guidance.

4. The Person Who Barges In

  • Answer: That person = the one who will cause you the most stress.
  • Reasoning: The intruder represents unexpected conflict or pressure, pointing to who (or what) creates tension in your life.
