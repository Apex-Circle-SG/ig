What Exactly Is the Repair 360 Leave-In Foam?

Unlike traditional creamy leave-in conditioners that can sometimes feel heavy or weigh down fine curl patterns, the Repair 360 Leave-In Foam utilizes an airy, lightweight delivery system. This formula is specifically designed to penetrate the hair shaft, providing hydration and structural support without the risk of product buildup or greasy residue. It targets damage at the cuticle level, making it an essential treatment for those who frequently use heat styling, color-treat their hair, or deal with daily environmental stressors.

Key Ingredients That Make a Difference

The efficacy of any hair product lies in its ingredient list. PATTERN has curated a blend that focuses on both immediate cosmetic improvement and long-term hair health. Key components include:

  • Rice Protein: Known for its ability to strengthen the hair shaft and improve elasticity, helping to prevent future breakage.
  • Biotin: A staple in hair health, biotin helps to fortify the hair follicle and promote stronger-looking strands.
  • Niacinamide: Often seen in skincare, this powerhouse ingredient helps improve the overall texture of hair and supports scalp health.
  • Marshmallow Root: Provides the signature slip that PATTERN fans adore, making detangling a breeze while soothing the scalp.

The Experience: Texture and Application

One of the most notable aspects of the Repair 360 Leave-In Foam is its sensory experience. The foam is incredibly lightweight, dissipating almost instantly into the hair. For those with Type 3 or Type 4 hair, this is a major advantage. Traditional creams often require a significant amount of product to ensure full saturation, which can lead to longer drying times. The foam, however, spreads effortlessly, ensuring that every coil is coated without the need for excessive product usage.

When applying the foam, you will notice an immediate cooling sensation, which is refreshing for the scalp. It is designed to be used on clean, damp hair, acting as a bridge between your wash day shampoo/conditioner and your styling products like gels or custards.

How to Incorporate It Into Your Routine

To get the most out of the Repair 360 Leave-In Foam, follow these steps:

  • Step 1: After washing and conditioning with your favorite PATTERN products, gently squeeze excess water from your hair.
  • Step 2: Dispense 2-3 pumps into your palms and work the foam through your hair from roots to ends.
  • Step 3: Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute evenly.
  • Step 4: Follow up with your preferred styling oil or gel to lock in the moisture and define your curl pattern.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Repair 360 Leave-In Foam contain protein?

Yes, the formula is infused with plant-based proteins that help repair and strengthen hair strands, which is ideal for hair that has been weakened by heat or chemical treatments.

Can I use this on dry hair?

While it is most effective when applied to damp hair to lock in hydration, it can be used on dry hair for a quick refresh or to tame flyaways. However, for maximum repair benefits, damp application is recommended.

Will this leave a sticky residue?

No, the foam is designed to absorb completely. It leaves the hair feeling soft and touchable rather than sticky or stiff, which is a common complaint with many styling foams on the market.

Is it safe for color-treated hair?

Absolutely. The formula is free from sulfates and parabens, making it safe for color-treated hair and gentle enough for daily use.

How often should I use it?

You can use the Repair 360 Leave-In Foam on every wash day. Its lightweight formula is designed for consistent use without causing product buildup, so you do not need to worry about over-saturating your hair.
