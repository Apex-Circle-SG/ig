Kokology Quiz: What Your Adventure as a Warrior Reveals About Pride, Confidence, and Attraction

2025-08-28

Quiz Questions

You are a warrior chosen to accompany a great hero on a quest to recover a stolen treasure.

  1. What does the hero say to you as you prepare to embark on your quest?
  2. The king gives you a sword—describe it in detail.
  3. You travel far, facing many obstacles and crises. How many enemies do you encounter?
  4. You finally arrive at your destination. What expression do you see on your hero’s face?

Answer Guide & Reasoning

1. What the hero says to you before the journey

  • Answer: Your pride weak spot.
  • Reasoning: The words you imagine reflect where your pride is most vulnerable and sensitive.

2. The sword given by the king

  • Answer: How you value your self-pride.
  • Reasoning: The sword’s design, strength, and quality mirror the way you perceive and protect your dignity.

3. The number of enemies you encountered

  • Answer: The greater the number, the more confidence you have in your abilities.
  • Reasoning: Enemies represent challenges, and counting them shows how much faith you place in your own strength.

4. The hero’s expression at the end

  • Answer: What you find most attractive in the opposite sex.
  • Reasoning: The hero’s face reflects the qualities you unconsciously admire and seek in others.
