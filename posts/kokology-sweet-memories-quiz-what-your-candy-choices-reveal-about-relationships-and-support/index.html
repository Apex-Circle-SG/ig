Kokology “Sweet Memories” Quiz: What Your Candy Choices Reveal About Relationships and Support

2025-08-25

Quiz Questions

Go back to an old sweet shop you remembered.

  1. What candy will you pick first, and why?
  2. How many children entered the sweet shop?
  3. The shopkeeper gave you how many extra sweets?
  4. You are giving the candy to someone—who is it?

Answer Guide & Reasoning

  1. Answer: The reason is how you judge who you want to mix with
    • Reasoning: Your first candy choice, and more importantly the reason behind it, reveals the criteria you use when choosing the people you want to connect and spend time with.
  2. Answer: How many people need your support
    • Reasoning: The number of children who walk into the sweet shop symbolizes how many people in your life look to you for guidance, care, or emotional support.
  3. Answer: How much you still depend on your mum
    • Reasoning: The extra sweets given by the shopkeeper reflect your subconscious relationship with maternal care and how much you continue to rely on your mother’s love or support in adulthood.
  4. Answer: Who is this special person
    • Reasoning: The person you choose to give candy to represents the individual you secretly cherish the most—the one who holds a very special place in your heart.
