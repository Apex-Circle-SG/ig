Advanced Features Driving Security and Compliance

The inclusion of Mitek on the FICO Marketplace brings a suite of advanced features designed to tackle modern threats. As regulatory bodies worldwide tighten requirements around Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML), having a platform that automatically adapts to these changes is crucial.

  • Document Authentication: The platform analyzes hundreds of data points on identity documents from over 200 countries, detecting forgeries, alterations, and digital manipulations with high precision.
  • Biometric Liveness Detection: To prevent spoofing attacks using photos or videos, Mitek employs passive liveness detection that ensures the user is a real person present at the time of verification.
  • Global Coverage: With support for thousands of document types globally, businesses can expand their reach without worrying about regional compliance gaps.
  • Regulatory Adaptability: The system is continuously updated to meet evolving standards such as GDPR, CCPA, and specific banking regulations, reducing the compliance burden on internal teams.

Combating Synthetic Identity Fraud

A growing threat in the digital economy is synthetic identity fraud, where criminals combine real and fake information to create new, fictitious identities. These frauds are notoriously difficult to detect because they often contain valid elements, such as a real Social Security number paired with a fake name. The integration of Mitek and FICO creates a powerful defense against this threat. Mitek verifies the physical document and the biometric match, while FICO’s analytics can cross-reference this data against vast networks of behavioral and credit data. This layered approach makes it exponentially harder for synthetic identities to pass through the verification funnel.
