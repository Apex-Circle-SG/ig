Bridging the Gap Between Clinics and Research

Historically, the data generated during pathology diagnostics has remained siloed within clinical environments. While biopsies and tissue samples are essential for identifying diseases, the immense wealth of biological data contained within these slides often goes underutilized in biopharmaceutical R&D. Proscia has effectively broken down these silos, moving beyond the traditional constraints of glass slides to a fully digital pathology ecosystem.

The winning platform enables researchers to bridge the gap between longitudinal patient data and deep biological insights. By turning every diagnostic instance into a data point, Proscia is empowering pharmaceutical teams to accelerate clinical trials, identify new therapeutic targets, and improve patient stratification in precision medicine.

The innovation that caught the attention of the MedTech Breakthrough committee is a sophisticated synergy of AI-driven computational pathology and cloud-based data management. AI-Powered Tissue Analysis

  • Automated pattern recognition: Identifying cellular features that are invisible to the naked eye.
  • Quantifiable biomarkers: Standardizing how diseases are measured, reducing inter-observer variability among pathologists.
  • Seamless integration: Connecting seamlessly with existing laboratory information systems to ensure zero disruption in diagnostic workflows.

Accelerating Drug Discovery Cycles

In the world of drug development, time is the most expensive commodity. Proscia’s platform reduces the time-to-insight by automating the manual elements of slide review.
