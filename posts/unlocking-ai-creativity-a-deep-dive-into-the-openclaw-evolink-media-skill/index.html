What is the Evolink Media Skill?

The Evolink Media skill is an integration for the OpenClaw ecosystem that grants you direct, API-powered access to high-tier generation tools for video, audio, and imagery. Instead of jumping between different platforms like Sora, Veo 3, or Suno, you handle your entire generation pipeline within one familiar interface. It abstracts the complexity of multiple API endpoints, providing a unified experience where you focus on the ‘what’ and the AI handles the ‘how’.

Key Features and Capabilities

The core philosophy of this skill is to provide a seamless, intent-first experience. By utilizing 6 specialized MCP tools, the skill can handle diverse creative requests:

  • Image Generation: Whether you need photorealistic assets or artistic illustrations, the integration supports a variety of models, including the cutting-edge GPT-4o-image. It allows for precise control over aspect ratios and iterative variations.
  • Video Production: Move beyond simple clips. With support for models like Seedance-1.5-pro, you can generate scenes, incorporate reference images, and even auto-generate accompanying audio for a complete cinematic feel.
  • Music Composition: The skill features a robust music generation suite that supports both simple and custom modes. You can define genres, moods, tempo, and even structure your song with specific markers for verses, bridges, and choruses.

How It Works: A Guided Experience

What sets the OpenClaw Evolink skill apart is its ‘Guide, Don’t Decide’ mantra. When you first install the skill, it does not overwhelm you with configuration options. It starts by verifying your API key and then asks a singular, focused question to determine your immediate needs. This context-aware approach ensures that you only provide the parameters that are strictly necessary for the specific task at hand, whether that is setting an aspect ratio for an image or choosing between instrumental and vocal tracks for your music.

The Power of Polling and Status Tracking

Generative AI tasks are inherently asynchronous. The Evolink Media skill handles this gracefully by utilizing a sophisticated polling mechanism. When you trigger a generation task, the system provides a task_id immediately and proactively updates you on progress via the check_task tool. It keeps you informed with percentage updates and reassures you during longer generation windows, ensuring you are never left wondering if your request is still being processed.

Best Practices for Users

To get the most out of this tool, keep a few critical guidelines in mind:

  • Intent First: The AI excels when you describe the scene or the feeling you want to capture. Avoid diving into technical parameters until you have clearly stated your creative vision.
  • Resource Management: Always remember that generated links are temporary. The system ensures you have the tools to track tasks, but you must download your assets within 24 hours to ensure you don’t lose your work.
  • Model Selection: If you are unsure which model fits your needs, don’t guess. Use the list_models feature to compare options based on speed, quality, or cost.
