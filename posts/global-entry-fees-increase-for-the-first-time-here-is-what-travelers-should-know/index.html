Global Entry Fees Increase for the First Time: Here is What Travelers Should Know

2026-04-26

Understanding the Global Entry Fee Hike

For millions of frequent international travelers, the Global Entry program has long been the gold standard for bypassing long lines at customs and immigration. However, after more than a decade of stagnant pricing, the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) has officially implemented a fee increase. Understanding these changes is essential for anyone looking to streamline their travel experience in the coming years.

As of late 2024, the application fee for Global Entry has increased from $100 to $120. This represents the first price adjustment since the program was launched in 2008. While no one enjoys paying more for government services, it is important to analyze why this hike occurred and how it compares to the overall value the program provides to busy global citizens.

Why Did the Fees Increase?

The decision to raise fees was not made lightly. The CBP cited several factors, including the need to modernize infrastructure, accommodate a surge in enrollment demand, and maintain the rigorous background check processes that keep the program secure. With technology evolving, the cost of maintaining secure databases and processing biometric data has grown significantly.

Furthermore, the increase aligns with the government’s goal of making the Trusted Traveler Programs (TTP) self-sustaining. By adjusting fees, the agency aims to reduce wait times for application processing and improve the overall user interface for the online portal, which has historically been a pain point for many applicants.

What Does Your Global Entry Membership Include?

Despite the price hike, the core benefits of the program remain unchanged. When you pay for Global Entry, you are not just paying for a fast track through customs. The membership includes several integrated perks that provide immense value for international travelers:

  • Expedited Customs Processing: Use automated kiosks to clear immigration in minutes rather than hours.
  • TSA PreCheck Benefits: Global Entry members automatically receive TSA PreCheck, allowing you to breeze through domestic security checkpoints without removing shoes, belts, or laptops.
  • Reduced Wait Times: You gain access to dedicated lanes at major international airports across the United States.
  • Global Reciprocity: The program is recognized by several partner countries, facilitating smoother entry procedures abroad.

Is Global Entry Still Worth the Investment?

For the average traveler, the math remains overwhelmingly in favor of Global Entry. At $120 for a five-year membership, the cost breaks down to just $24 per year. When you consider the time saved on a single international trip, combined with the convenience of TSA PreCheck on every domestic flight, the value proposition is clear.

If you take even one or two international trips per year, the time saved at customs is worth far more than the $24 annual amortized cost. Moreover, many premium credit cards now offer fee reimbursement for Global Entry applications. If you hold a travel-oriented credit card, check your benefits portal; you may be able to get this fee covered entirely by your card issuer.

How to Apply for Global Entry

The application process remains digital-first. Travelers must create an account on the official TTP website, fill out a detailed application, and pay the non-refundable fee. Once conditionally approved, you must schedule an interview at an enrollment center. A newer feature, Enrollment on Arrival, allows travelers to complete their interview while clearing customs after an international flight, which has significantly reduced the need for separate appointments.

Tips for a Smooth Application Process

  • Double-check your information: Any errors in your passport number or biographical data can lead to immediate denial or significant delays.
  • Be prepared for the interview: Bring your physical passport and an additional form of identification, such as a driver’s license.
  • Use credit card perks: Always check if your credit card covers the fee before you submit your payment.
  • Monitor your status: Log into your TTP account periodically to check for updates on your conditional approval status.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the fee increase apply to children?

Yes, the $120 fee applies to all applicants regardless of age. Every individual, including infants and children, must have their own separate Global Entry membership to use the expedited lanes.

Will my existing membership cost more?

No, if you are already a member, your current membership remains valid until its expiration date. The fee increase only applies to new applications and renewals submitted after the policy change went into effect.

Can I still get the fee reimbursed by my credit card?

In most cases, yes. Most credit cards that previously offered a $100 credit for Global Entry have updated their benefits to cover the new $120 fee. It is highly recommended to verify this with your specific card issuer before applying.

How long does the approval process take?

Processing times fluctuate based on current application volumes. While some applicants receive conditional approval in just a few days, others may wait several months. It is best to apply well in advance of any planned international travel.

Is Global Entry the same as NEXUS or SENTRI?

While they are all part of the Trusted Traveler Programs, they serve different purposes. Global Entry is designed for international air travel. NEXUS is specifically for travel between the U.S. and Canada, while SENTRI is for land border crossings between the U.S. and Mexico. However, Global Entry members often enjoy some benefits of these other programs at specific locations.

Final Thoughts

While the increase to $120 may be an adjustment, the efficiency and peace of mind provided by Global Entry remain unmatched for the modern traveler. By planning ahead, leveraging credit card benefits, and understanding the application requirements, you can ensure that your travel experiences remain as smooth and stress-free as possible. Do not let the price change deter you; the value of reclaiming your time at the airport is well worth the investment.
