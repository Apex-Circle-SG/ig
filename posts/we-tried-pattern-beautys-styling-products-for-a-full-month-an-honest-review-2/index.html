We Tried Pattern Beauty’s Styling Products for a Full Month: An Honest Review

2026-05-17

Introduction to Pattern Beauty

For those of us with curly, coily, and tight-textured hair, the search for the holy grail of styling products is a never-ending journey. When Tracee Ellis Ross launched Pattern Beauty, the beauty world took notice. The brand promised to embrace and nourish natural textures rather than trying to tame or alter them. After seeing countless testimonials, we decided to put the styling line to the ultimate test: a full 30-day trial. Did it hold up, or was it just celebrity-branded hype? Here is our comprehensive review.

The Core Philosophy Behind Pattern Beauty

Pattern Beauty isn’t just another hair brand; it is a movement focused on curl patterns being the defining characteristic of the hair. The range is designed to provide hydration, definition, and strengthening. During our month-long trial, we focused on three key products: the Styling Cream, the Strong Hold Gel, and the Leave-In Conditioner. Each product aims to address common issues like dryness, frizz, and lack of definition.

Week 1: The First Impression

We started the month by detoxing our hair and moving into the Pattern routine. The first thing we noticed was the scent—it is sophisticated, floral, and subtle, not overpowering like many synthetic hair products. The Leave-In Conditioner is the workhorse of the brand. It has a rich, creamy consistency that instantly melted into our strands. Even in the first week, the hydration levels were noticeably higher than our usual salon-brand products.

Week 2: Mastering the Styling Cream

During the second week, we introduced the Styling Cream. We found that the key to success with Pattern is the amount used. A little goes a long way. When applied to damp, detangled hair, it provided a soft, touchable hold. For those who prefer a ‘wash-and-go’ style, this product allows for movement without the crunch. Our curls felt bouncy and looked significantly healthier under direct sunlight.

Week 3: Tackling Frizz with the Strong Hold Gel

The middle of the month brought high humidity, the ultimate test for any styling line. We incorporated the Strong Hold Gel for our protective styles and definition-heavy days. Unlike typical drugstore gels that leave flakes or a straw-like texture, this gel provided a firm grip while maintaining a glossy finish. We were particularly impressed by how it held up during an intense gym session without causing build-up.

Week 4: Long-Term Results and Scalp Health

By the end of the month, the changes were undeniable. Our curl health had improved significantly. The constant moisture infusion from the styling products minimized breakage during the detangling process. We found that our hair retained water longer, reducing the need for daily refreezing of the curls. The scalp irritation we usually experience after two weeks of product build-up was non-existent, likely due to the cleaner ingredient profiles in the Pattern range.

Pros and Cons Analysis

No product line is perfect for everyone. Here is what we discovered after thirty days of daily usage.

  • Pros: Excellent slip for detangling, high moisture retention, beautiful aesthetic packaging, and ingredients formulated specifically for coily hair.
  • Cons: The price point can be a hurdle for some users, and the containers are sometimes challenging to navigate in the shower with wet hands.

Key Ingredients to Watch

The efficacy of these products comes down to the formulation. Pattern Beauty emphasizes the use of rich emollients. You will find ingredients like Shea Butter, Honey, and Aloe Vera across the collection. Shea Butter provides the deep conditioning needed for coarser curls, while Honey acts as a humectant to draw moisture into the shaft. Aloe Vera serves as a soothing agent for the scalp, which is a game-changer for those who suffer from dryness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pattern Beauty suitable for all hair types?

Pattern Beauty is explicitly formulated for curly, coily, and tight-textured hair (types 3 and 4). While those with wavy hair might find the products hydrating, they may be too heavy for finer hair textures.

Does the Strong Hold Gel leave a crunchy cast?

While the gel provides a firm hold for definition, it does not leave hair feeling brittle. If you experience a ‘cast,’ simply fluff your curls once they are completely dry to reveal soft, defined coils.

How often should I use the Styling Cream?

We used the Styling Cream every time we refreshed our curls. It is lightweight enough for daily use, but we recommend clarifying your hair once every two weeks to prevent silicones and heavy oils from accumulating.

Is this line vegan-friendly?

Yes, Pattern Beauty is proudly cruelty-free and many of their products are vegan. Always check the individual product labels if you have specific sensitivities or requirements.

Final Verdict: Should You Buy It?

After a full month, our final consensus is a resounding yes. Pattern Beauty succeeds because it understands the unique hydration needs of natural textured hair. It isn’t just about styling; it is about building a foundation of health. If you are looking for products that honor your natural pattern while helping you achieve salon-quality results at home, the investment is well worth it. Your curls will thank you for the extra moisture and the refined definition.
