Who Is Tony Ressler?

Tony Ressler is a titan in the world of private equity. He is the co-founder of Ares Management, a global alternative asset manager with hundreds of billions of dollars in assets under management. His financial prowess has earned him a place on the billionaire lists, but he is perhaps best known by the general public as the majority owner of the Atlanta Hawks, the NBA franchise he purchased in 2015.

Ressler’s approach to business is characterized by a long-term vision, a trait that seems to mirror his approach to his personal life and marriage. His professional success has provided the foundation for the couple’s extensive philanthropic endeavors, which are a cornerstone of their shared identity.

The Power Couple Dynamic
