Kokology Quiz: What Your Jewelry Stall Reveals About Your Social Masks and Relationships

2025-08-26

Quiz Questions

Imagine you’re at a lively fair filled with exquisite boutiques and merchandise. You decide to open your own stall for handmade jewelry.

  1. How many different types of accessories and bangles do you put on display?
  2. Why do you think someone will walk over and leave without buying anything?
  3. What type of salesmanship will you use to close a potential customer?
  4. How would you describe your sales when the shop closes?

Answer Guide & Reasoning

1. How many different types of accessories and bangles do you put on display?

  • Answer: How many social masks you will put on.
  • Reasoning: The number of accessories reflects how many roles or personas you use when dealing with different people in life.

2. Why do you think someone will walk over and leave without buying anything?

  • Answer: How you feel after a heartbreak or rejection.
  • Reasoning: Your interpretation of a customer’s departure mirrors how you rationalize or cope with emotional rejection in real life.

3. What type of salesmanship will you use to close a potential customer?

  • Answer: How you express your strong points to others.
  • Reasoning: The way you persuade and present your work reflects the manner in which you showcase your strengths in social and personal settings.

4. How would you describe your sales when the shop closes?

  • Answer: Your social achievement.
  • Reasoning: The way you summarize your performance mirrors how you measure success and accomplishments in your social life.
