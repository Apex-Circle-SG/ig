Kokology Quiz: Just Can’t Wait – What Your Friend’s Mistake Reveals About Your Personality

2025-08-28

Quiz Question

You visit your friend who has a new job, but he is having some kind of trouble. What did he do wrong?

  1. Didn’t come to the table after repeated calling
  2. Made a mistake in order taking
  3. Spilled something on the customer’s clothes
  4. Cleared the table before the customer finished

Answer Guide & Reasoning

1. Didn’t come to the table after repeated calling – You lack ability to concentrate

  • Reasoning: Overlooking or forgetting tasks in this scenario reflects difficulty focusing and maintaining attention in your own life.

2. Made a mistake in order taking – Always being late or bringing uninvited friends

  • Reasoning: Errors due to disorganization mirror a tendency to be inattentive to schedules or social expectations.

3. Spilled something on the customer’s clothes – You are a generally serious person

  • Reasoning: Accidents in this context reflect how seriously you approach responsibilities and how disruptions can affect your sense of control.

4. Cleared the table before the customer finished – You are over eager

  • Reasoning: Acting too quickly or anticipating outcomes shows your impatience and tendency to rush situations without waiting for proper timing.
