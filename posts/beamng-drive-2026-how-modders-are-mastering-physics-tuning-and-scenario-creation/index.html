FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What makes BeamNG.drive physics so special?

BeamNG.drive uses a unique soft-body physics engine that simulates the weight, structural integrity, and physical properties of every individual component of a vehicle. Unlike traditional games that use simplified hitboxes, BeamNG calculates the deformation of the chassis and suspension in real-time.

How can I start learning to tune physics in BeamNG?

The best way to start is by exploring the existing JBeam files in the game’s repository. By modifying simple values like spring stiffness or tire grip and testing the results in the game, you can learn how these parameters interact with the engine. The official BeamNG wiki is also an invaluable resource for beginners.

Are these advanced mods compatible with all game versions?

While most mods are designed to be compatible with the current version of the game, major updates can sometimes break older mods. Always check the mod’s release notes and ensure you are running the latest version of the game to avoid stability issues.

Where can I find the best scenarios in 2026?

The official BeamNG mod repository remains the safest and most reliable source for high-quality content. Additionally, community forums and Discord servers are excellent places to find beta-tested scenarios and early-access mods from top creators.
