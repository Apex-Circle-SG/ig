Introduction to the Pattern Blow Dryer

When Tracee Ellis Ross launched Pattern Beauty, it immediately became a staple for those with curly, coily, and tight-textured hair. The brand has been celebrated for its commitment to moisture, definition, and health. So, when the brand announced the release of the Pattern Blow Dryer, the beauty community held its breath. Could a hot tool really be designed with the specific needs of textured hair in mind? We decided to put it to the test. In this review, we break down our experience, the features, and whether this tool is the missing piece in your haircare routine.

Design and Ergonomics

The first thing you notice about the Pattern Blow Dryer is its sleek, minimalist aesthetic. Unlike many bulky salon-style dryers, this unit feels lightweight and well-balanced. For those with thick hair, the drying process can take a significant amount of time, often leading to arm fatigue. The ergonomic design of this dryer is a game-changer. It feels comfortable in the hand, and the buttons are placed intuitively, making it easy to toggle between heat and speed settings without accidentally shutting it off.

The Attachment Ecosystem

What truly sets this dryer apart is its focus on attachments. Pattern includes a variety of tools designed to cater to different curl patterns:

  • The Diffuser: A large, deep bowl that is perfect for distributing heat evenly without disturbing the curl pattern.
  • The Concentrator: Ideal for precision styling and smoothing out roots.
  • The Wide-Tooth Comb: A critical addition for detangling while drying, which is often a point of breakage for many.
  • The Brush Attachment: Great for those who want a blowout look without needing a separate round brush.

Performance and Heat Settings

The Pattern Blow Dryer utilizes advanced heat technology that prioritizes hair health. We found that the heat distribution is incredibly consistent. Often, dryers suffer from hot spots that can cause heat damage or singe delicate strands. This tool maintains a steady temperature that effectively dries the hair without stripping it of its natural moisture. The airflow is powerful enough to reduce drying time significantly, which is a massive plus for anyone with high-density hair.

Customization for Your Curl Pattern

The dryer features multiple heat and speed settings, allowing for a customized experience. We recommend starting on a lower heat setting if you are diffusing, as this helps prevent frizz and maintains the integrity of your curl pattern. For those aiming for a straight blowout, the higher heat settings paired with the brush attachment provide a professional-quality finish that rivals salon results.

The Results: Is It Worth It?

After using the tool for several weeks, we noticed a significant improvement in the manageability of our hair. The inclusion of ionic technology helps to reduce static and flyaways, leaving the hair looking shiny and healthy. Unlike other high-end dryers that focus solely on heat, the Pattern tool feels like an extension of the brand’s philosophy: hair health first. While the price point is premium, the combination of durability, specialized attachments, and hair-friendly technology makes it a worthwhile investment for anyone who styles their hair regularly.

Maintenance and Care

To ensure your investment lasts, regular maintenance is key. The removable filter at the back of the dryer is easy to access, which allows you to clear out dust and hair buildup easily. Keeping this area clean is essential for maintaining the dryer’s power and preventing overheating. We recommend a monthly check-up to ensure optimal performance.

When you compare the Pattern Blow Dryer to other professional options on the market, the primary differentiator is the intentionality behind the attachments. Many universal dryers provide attachments that feel like afterthoughts. With Pattern, every attachment feels like it was crafted to address a specific pain point in the textured hair community. From the wide-tooth comb attachment to the deep-bowl diffuser, it is clear that the design team listened to the needs of their customers.

The Pattern diffuser is significantly deeper than standard models. This extra depth allows for more hair to be cradled at once, which promotes faster drying and better volume while minimizing frizz.

Can I use this dryer for a professional blowout?

Absolutely. When used with the concentrator or the brush attachment, this dryer provides enough airflow and heat control to achieve a sleek, smooth blowout that looks like it was done in a salon.
