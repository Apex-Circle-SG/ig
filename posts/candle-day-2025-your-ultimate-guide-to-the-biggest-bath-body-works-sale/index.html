Candle Day 2025: Your Ultimate Guide to the Biggest Bath & Body Works Sale

2026-05-11

What is Candle Day?

For fragrance enthusiasts and home decor lovers, Candle Day is widely considered the most anticipated shopping event of the year. Hosted annually by Bath & Body Works, this event features the brand’s iconic three-wick candles at their lowest prices of the season. Thousands of shoppers flock to both brick-and-mortar stores and the official website to stock up on seasonal scents, exclusive releases, and long-time favorites.

When is Candle Day 2025?

While Bath & Body Works has not officially released the exact dates for Candle Day 2025, the event traditionally takes place during the first weekend of December. Historically, the sale kicks off on a Friday and extends through the weekend, often continuing until Sunday or until supplies last. Savvy shoppers should keep a close eye on their email inbox and the official mobile app starting in late November to catch early access announcements.

What to Expect from Candle Day 2025 Pricing

The core appeal of Candle Day is the deeply discounted price point. In recent years, the price per three-wick candle has hovered near the 10-dollar mark. While prices can fluctuate due to inflation and supply chain factors, you can expect a significant discount compared to the standard retail price of 26.95 dollars or higher. Many shoppers use this event as an opportunity to purchase gifts for the entire year, including birthdays, holidays, and housewarming presents.

How to Prepare for the Big Event

Preparation is the key to a stress-free shopping experience. Because inventory moves incredibly fast, having a plan is essential. Follow these expert tips to maximize your haul:

  • Sign up for the Loyalty Program: Join the Bath & Body Works rewards program well in advance to receive exclusive notifications, early access codes, and potential bonus coupons.
  • Download the App: The mobile app is often the fastest way to checkout during high-traffic periods. Make sure your payment information and shipping address are saved in advance.
  • Create a Wishlist: Browsing the entire catalog during the sale can be overwhelming. Create a list of your top-priority scents so you can add them to your cart immediately upon launch.
  • Budget Accordingly: Decide on a maximum spend limit before you start shopping to avoid impulse buys during the excitement of the sale.

Shopping In-Store vs. Online

The In-Store Experience

Shopping in a physical store offers the benefit of smelling the candles before you buy. However, be prepared for long lines and crowded aisles. Many stores open early on Friday morning, and dedicated fans often arrive hours before opening to ensure they get the best selection. If you plan to go in person, bring a friend to help carry your haul and make the wait more enjoyable.

The Online Experience

Online shopping offers the convenience of browsing from home, but it comes with its own set of challenges. Site traffic can lead to virtual queues and slower load times. To improve your chances of getting your favorites, log in to your account five minutes before the sale goes live. Have your credit card information ready, as items in your cart are not reserved until you complete the checkout process.

Frequently Asked Questions About Candle Day

How low will the prices be during Candle Day 2025?

While Bath & Body Works keeps final pricing under wraps until the event begins, history suggests that prices will be significantly reduced, likely near the 10-dollar per candle range for standard three-wick varieties.

Can I use coupons during Candle Day?

Bath & Body Works typically restricts the use of additional coupons during Candle Day because the prices are already at their absolute lowest. However, it is always a good idea to check the fine print on any coupons you have, as policies can change from year to year.

Will there be exclusive scents released for the event?

Yes, one of the most exciting aspects of Candle Day is the release of limited-edition scents that are only available during this event. These candles often feature unique packaging and festive themes that disappear quickly.

How long does the sale last?

The sale generally lasts for the duration of the weekend, but popular scents often sell out within the first few hours. It is highly recommended to shop as early as possible to ensure you get the items you want.

What should I do if my favorite scent is out of stock?

If you miss out on a scent, keep checking the website. Bath & Body Works often restocks inventory throughout the weekend. Additionally, you can check local stores using the store locator feature on the website to see if nearby locations still have stock available.

Conclusion: Is Candle Day Worth the Hype?

For those who love home fragrance, Candle Day 2025 is absolutely worth the effort. By preparing ahead of time, understanding the typical price points, and staying flexible with your scent choices, you can secure a year’s worth of high-quality candles for a fraction of the cost. Whether you are shopping for yourself or checking off your holiday gift list, this event is the ultimate destination for value and variety in the world of home fragrance.
