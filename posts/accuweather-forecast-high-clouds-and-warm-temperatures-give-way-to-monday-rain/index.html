  • Reviewing home gutter systems if you live in an area prone to heavy localized rainfall.

Comparative Look: Why Weather Patterns Change So Quickly

Understanding why we experience these “rollercoaster” weather patterns can help in planning for the future. Meteorologists at AccuWeather point to the jet stream—a fast-moving river of air high in the atmosphere. When the jet stream dips southward, it brings colder, wetter air; when it pushes northward, it allows warmer, drier air to dominate.

Currently, we are caught in the transition zone of these shifts. A northward nudge is giving us this warm weekend, while a southward shift beginning Monday is pulling in the moisture responsible for the upcoming rain. This volatility is a hallmark of this time of year.
