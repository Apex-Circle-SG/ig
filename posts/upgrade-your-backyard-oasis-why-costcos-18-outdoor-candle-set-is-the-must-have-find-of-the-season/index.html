Design Tips to Style Your New Candles

Buying the candles is only the first step. To truly maximize the impact of your new purchase, consider these professional styling tips to make your patio look like a high-end resort:

  • Create Layers: Do not just place the candles in a straight line. Group them in odd numbers—threes and fives work best—at varying heights to create visual interest.
  • Pair with Natural Elements: Place your candle set on a wooden tray or alongside potted succulents to ground the look and enhance the organic feel of your outdoor space.
  • Incorporate Textiles: Soften the look of your hard patio surfaces by placing your candles near outdoor pillows or a cozy throw blanket draped over a chair.
  • Safe Placement: Always ensure your candles are placed on stable, non-flammable surfaces and keep them away from overhanging branches or outdoor fabrics that could catch fire.
