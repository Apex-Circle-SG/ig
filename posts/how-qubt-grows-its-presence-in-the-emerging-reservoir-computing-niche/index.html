Before delving into QUBT’s strategies, it’s essential to understand what reservoir computing is. Reservoir computing is a computational framework derived from recurrent neural networks, designed to handle temporal data and complex dynamic systems. Its applications range from time-series prediction to speech recognition, making it a versatile tool in the tech industry.
