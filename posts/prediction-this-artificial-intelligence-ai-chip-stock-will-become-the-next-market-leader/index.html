Why Specialized AI Hardware Matters

To understand why a new player is poised to emerge as a market leader, one must first understand the limitations of current hardware. General-purpose CPUs were never designed to handle the massive parallel processing requirements of deep learning models. The current market standard relies on Graphical Processing Units (GPUs) that have been retrofitted for AI workloads. However, the future belongs to custom-built Application-Specific Integrated Circuits (ASICs). These chips are purpose-built for AI, offering superior power efficiency and lower latency compared to traditional hardware.
