MGE Energy Announces Strategic Leadership Update: Jared Bushek Appointed Executive Vice President and CFO

2026-04-22

A New Era of Financial Leadership at MGE Energy

In a significant move for the Madison-based energy provider, MGE Energy has announced the promotion of Jared Bushek to the dual roles of Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer. This leadership transition marks a pivotal moment for the organization as it continues to navigate the complexities of the modern utility landscape, focusing on sustainability, grid reliability, and shareholder value.

Jared Bushek, who has been a cornerstone of the financial operations team at MGE Energy for several years, brings a wealth of experience to his new position. His promotion reflects the company’s commitment to internal succession planning and its confidence in his ability to steer the financial future of the corporation during a time of significant technological and environmental transformation.

Understanding the Role of the CFO in the Energy Sector

The role of a Chief Financial Officer in a publicly traded utility company is multifaceted. Unlike traditional industries, the energy sector is heavily regulated and requires a delicate balance between capital investment in infrastructure and maintaining affordable rates for consumers. Jared Bushek will be responsible for overseeing the company’s financial planning, risk management, and investor relations, ensuring that MGE Energy remains financially robust while transitioning toward cleaner energy sources.

Strategic Financial Planning

As the energy industry shifts away from fossil fuels, utilities must make massive capital expenditures to incorporate renewable energy sources like wind and solar. Bushek’s expertise will be critical in securing the necessary funding and managing the debt-to-equity ratios that influence the company’s credit rating and overall stability.

Regulatory Compliance and Stakeholder Relations

Utility companies operate under strict oversight from public service commissions. A major part of the CFO’s responsibility involves working closely with regulatory bodies to justify rate cases and ensure that investments in the grid are approved and transparent. Bushek’s background positions him well to manage these complex interactions, ensuring that MGE Energy maintains its reputation as a reliable and transparent utility provider.

The Career Path of Jared Bushek

Before ascending to the role of Executive Vice President, Jared Bushek served in various high-level capacities within the MGE finance department. His deep institutional knowledge of the company’s operations, combined with his strategic vision for long-term fiscal health, made him the ideal candidate for this promotion. Colleagues and industry peers often cite his analytical prowess and his ability to communicate complex financial data to non-financial stakeholders as key strengths.

  • Experience: Over a decade of progressive financial leadership within the energy sector.
  • Expertise: Specialization in capital structure optimization, regulatory accounting, and risk mitigation.
  • Vision: A strong commitment to supporting MGE Energy’s goal of net-zero carbon emissions by 2050 through disciplined financial management.
