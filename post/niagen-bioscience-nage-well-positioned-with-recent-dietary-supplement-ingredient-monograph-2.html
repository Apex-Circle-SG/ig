Understanding the Role of Ingredient Monographs

An ingredient monograph serves as a gold-standard reference document that outlines the identity, purity, safety, and recommended usage of a specific substance. When a regulatory body or an authoritative industry group releases a monograph, it effectively sets the standard for how that ingredient should be manufactured, tested, and labeled. For Niagen Bioscience, this creates a high barrier to entry for competitors who cannot meet these rigorous standards.

The Science Behind Niagen Bioscience and NAD+

To appreciate why the monograph is so significant, one must first understand the importance of NAD+ in the human body.
