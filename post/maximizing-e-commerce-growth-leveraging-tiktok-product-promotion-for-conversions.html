Creating Effective Product Promotion Campaigns

For maximum ROI, structure campaigns around these key elements:

Element Importance Best Practices
Product Selection High Choose products aligned with influencer’s audience and expertise
Clear CTAs Critical Include specific instructions for links, codes, and purchasing options
Compensation Structure High Offer base pay plus performance-based incentives like commissions
Tracking Metrics Essential Track conversions, sales, and ROI using TikTok’s built-in analytics

Success Metrics for Product Promotion

When evaluating campaigns, focus on:

  • Conversion Rate: The percentage of viewers who purchase after watching
  • Sales per Video: The number of units or revenue generated from one post
  • Click-Through Rate: How many people follow links to your product
  • Customer Lifetime Value: Long-term impact of the promotion on repeat purchases
  • Return on Ad Spend: How much revenue each dollar of payment generates
