How to Get the Most Out of Your Better Homes & Gardens Candle

Even the best candles require proper maintenance to ensure they last as long as possible. To maximize your purchase and keep your home smelling amazing, consider these expert tips:

  • Trim your wick: Always trim the wick to about 1/4 inch before each burn. This prevents the candle from smoking and ensures a clean, steady flame.
  • The initial burn: Make sure your first burn lasts long enough for the wax to melt all the way to the edges of the jar. This prevents tunneling, which can waste a significant amount of wax.
  • Avoid drafts: Keep your candle away from fans, open windows, or air conditioning vents to prevent uneven burning and soot production.
  • Safety first: Never leave a burning candle unattended, and keep it away from pets, children, or flammable materials.
