Defining the Agentic AI Shift

Unlike traditional AI models that rely on human prompts for every specific task, Agentic AI possesses the autonomy to plan, reason, and execute multi-step objectives within defined parameters. For the CDAO, this means moving beyond simple data processing to creating intelligent systems that can navigate complex digital environments, perform autonomous cybersecurity monitoring, and streamline logistical decision-making in real-time.
