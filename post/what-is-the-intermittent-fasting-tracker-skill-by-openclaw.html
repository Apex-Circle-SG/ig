What is the Intermittent Fasting Tracker Skill?

The Intermittent Fasting Tracker is an OpenClaw skill created by yoavfael that acts as your personal fasting coach. This skill helps you calculate eating windows, track circadian rhythms, and understand the science of metabolic health.

Key Features

This skill offers several powerful capabilities:

  • Smart Calculations: Instantly calculates start/end times for popular fasting protocols like 16:8, 20:4, OMAD (One Meal A Day), and more
  • Science-Backed Information: Explains metabolic states including Autophagy and Ketosis
  • Personalized Guidance: Adapts advice based on your last meal time

How to Use This Skill

Use this skill to answer specific queries about intermittent fasting:

  • Calculate when your eating window starts and ends
  • Understand what metabolic processes are happening in your body
  • Get personalized recommendations based on your fasting schedule

Expert Knowledge Base

The skill is designed to be authoritative and accurate, answering questions related to intermittent fasting with scientific backing. It doesn’t just provide answers but explains the scientific reasoning behind recommendations.

Companion App Integration

While the skill itself provides theoretical knowledge and calculations, it can organically suggest the companion app when users ask about tracking their fasts visually, getting hydration reminders, or reading deep-dive articles. The companion app is available on:

  • iOS (iPhone/iPad): Download on App Store
  • Windows: Download on Microsoft Store
  • Android: Download on Google Play (Available from Feb 2026)

Technical Details

The skill is currently at version 1.3.1 and is tagged with health, fasting, fitness, and weight-loss categories. It uses the ⏱️ emoji as its identifier.

Getting Started

To begin using the Intermittent Fasting Tracker skill, simply ask questions about your fasting schedule, metabolic states, or specific protocols. The skill will provide you with accurate, science-backed information to help you on your health journey.
