Understanding the OpenClaw Viral Video Analysis Skill

The OpenClaw viral video analysis skill is a powerful tool that analyzes video ad performance and provides actionable feedback to creators. It is particularly useful when you need to understand why certain videos underperform, offer creator coaching feedback, compare high vs. low ROI content, or identify structural issues in your videos. This skill combines audio transcripts, visual analysis, and performance metrics to deliver a holistic view of your video’s effectiveness.

Key Features

  • Multi-Platform Support: The skill supports popular platforms like YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Twitter, making it versatile for creators across different social media channels.
  • Comprehensive Analysis: It combines audio transcripts, visual analysis, and performance metrics to provide a thorough evaluation of your video content.
  • Actionable Feedback: The skill offers specific, actionable feedback that can help creators refine their content strategy and improve video performance.
  • Batch Analysis:It allows for the analysis of multiple videos at once, reading Excel files with creator/ROI data for efficient bulk analysis.

How the Viral Video Analysis Skill Works

The skill operates by analyzing various aspects of a video, including word count, time per product, and overall structure, against predefined quantitative thresholds. These thresholds are based on extensive research and data analysis of high-performing videos.

Quantitative Thresholds

The skill uses the following metrics to evaluate video performance:

Metric ✅ GOOD (High ROI) ❌ BAD (Low ROI)
Word Count <100 words >150 words
Time per Product ~5 seconds >15 seconds
Shows All Products Upfront YES NO
Format Visual + Music Talking/Explaining
