What is the ErrandAI Skill?

At its core, the ErrandAI skill is an extension for the OpenClaw ecosystem that connects your AI assistant directly to the ErrandAI platform. ErrandAI operates as a decentralized task marketplace where individuals can accept micro-tasks, and you, as the requester, can set specific parameters, rewards, and deadlines. By installing this skill, your OpenClaw assistant transforms from a text-based chatbot into an actionable project manager capable of deploying human labor across the globe.

Key Features and Capabilities

The primary advantage of this skill lies in its versatility and ease of use. Below are the standout features that make it an essential tool for any power user:

  • Natural Language Commands: Forget about complex syntax. You can simply tell your AI, ‘Post an errand to check the price of coffee at the local cafe,’ and the skill handles the heavy lifting.
  • Status Tracking: Keep tabs on your ongoing tasks in real-time. Whether you are checking the progress of a stock inquiry or a document translation, the status updates are instantly retrievable.
  • Work Review System: Quality control is paramount. The skill includes a built-in approval workflow, allowing you to review submissions, offer feedback, and reject or approve work before any funds change hands.
  • Automated USDC Payments: Transparency and speed are guaranteed through Web3 integration. Once you approve a submission, the payment in USDC is released automatically, ensuring a frictionless experience for both you and the worker.
  • Global Reach: Because the ErrandAI marketplace is decentralized, your errands are not limited by geography. You can post tasks for any location worldwide, making it perfect for international research or location-specific verification.

Setting Up Your Environment

Installing the ErrandAI skill is straightforward, provided you have the right prerequisites. Before beginning, ensure you are running OpenClaw v1.0.0 or higher and Node.js v14.0.0 or higher. You will also need an API key, which you can obtain from the official ErrandAI dashboard.

To install, run the command openclaw skill install errand-ai in your terminal. For those who prefer a manual touch, you can clone the files directly into your ~/.openclaw/skills/ directory. After installation, you must configure your API key as an environment variable to ensure secure communication with the ErrandAI servers.
