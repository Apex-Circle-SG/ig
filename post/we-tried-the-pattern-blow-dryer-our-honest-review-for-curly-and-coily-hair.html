Key Features and Attachments

A blow dryer is only as good as its attachments, especially when dealing with textured hair. Pattern Beauty has clearly done their research, providing a suite of tools designed to handle different styling goals.

  • The Diffuser: This is arguably the star of the show. It is wide and deep, allowing it to cradle large sections of hair without frizzing them out. It promotes even airflow, ensuring that your roots dry just as efficiently as your ends.
  • The Concentrator Nozzle: Essential for those who want a smooth, straight look. It focuses the airflow, making it easier to work with a brush for a professional-grade blowout.
  • The Wide-Tooth Comb: A game-changer for those with tight coils who need to stretch their hair from the root. It helps detangle and dry simultaneously, saving significant time during the styling process.
  • The Brush Attachment: Perfect for smoothing out the hair shaft and achieving a polished finish without needing to juggle a separate brush and dryer.

Performance on Different Curl Patterns

We tested the Pattern Blow Dryer on a variety of textures, from 3B waves to 4C coils. Here is how it performed across the board.

For those with looser curl patterns, the diffuser attachment worked wonders. It managed to dry the hair quickly without blowing the curl definition apart. The result was voluminous, bouncy curls that looked like they had been professionally styled.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Pattern Blow Dryer safe for chemically treated hair?

Yes, the dryer features multiple heat settings, including a lower heat option that is safer for color-treated or chemically relaxed hair. We recommend always starting on the lowest heat setting and using a heat protectant spray to maintain the health of your hair.

Does this dryer cause frizz?

When used correctly with the appropriate attachment, the Pattern Blow Dryer is designed to minimize frizz. Using the diffuser with a low airflow setting is the best way to ensure your curls remain defined and frizz-free. If you are aiming for a straight look, ensure you are using the concentrator nozzle in a downward motion to seal the hair cuticle.

Can I use this dryer for a professional-style blowout at home?

Absolutely. With the included concentrator nozzle and brush attachment, you have all the tools necessary to achieve a salon-quality blowout. The key is to work in small sections and maintain consistent tension with your brush for the smoothest results.

How does it compare to the Dyson Supersonic?

While the Dyson is known for its high-tech motor and extreme speed, the Pattern Blow Dryer is specifically optimized for the needs of textured hair. Its attachments are better suited for stretching coils and diffusing dense curls, making it a more practical choice for many within the natural hair community.

Is the dryer heavy to hold?

We found the weight to be well-distributed. Even for those with a high volume of hair who require 30 to 45 minutes of drying time, the ergonomic design helps prevent wrist fatigue. It feels balanced and comfortable in the hand throughout the entire styling session.
