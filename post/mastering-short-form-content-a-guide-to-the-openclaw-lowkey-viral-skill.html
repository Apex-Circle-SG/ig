What Exactly is the Lowkey Viral Skill?

The Lowkey Viral skill is an automated content generation tool designed specifically for the OpenClaw ecosystem. It allows users to leverage AI-powered video synthesis to create short-form media without needing to manually edit every clip. Whether you are aiming to produce a 5-second, 2×2 grid video for TikTok or a multi-slide photo carousel for an Instagram educational post, this skill streamlines the process from initial prompt to finished render.

Key Features and Capabilities

This skill provides two primary methods for content creation, catering to different viral formats:

1. AI-Powered Grid Videos

The skill excels at generating 1080×1920 vertical MP4 videos that are perfectly optimized for mobile feeds. By utilizing a 2×2 image grid, it creates a fast-paced viewing experience that keeps users engaged. Features include:

  • Customizable Layouts: Choose between designs like ‘default’, ‘withCaptions’, and ‘noSpaces’ to match your brand identity.
  • AI-Generated Imagery: Use powerful models like ‘z_image_turbo’, ‘p_image’, or ‘flux_2_dev’ to generate custom visuals directly from your prompts.
  • Integrated Soundtracks: Enhance your videos with access to 20 CC0-licensed background music tracks.

2. High-Engagement Photo Slideshows

Photo carousels are currently one of the most effective ways to drive engagement on Instagram and TikTok. The Lowkey Viral skill makes generating these slideshows simple. You can create between 2 and 10 portrait slides with various caption styles, such as ‘classic_bold’, ‘background_bar’, or ‘neon_glow’, ensuring your messaging remains clear and visually appealing across all slides.

How the Workflow Works

The beauty of this skill lies in its structured workflow, which relies on the Lowkey Viral REST API. The process generally follows a four-step pipeline:

  1. Generate AI Briefs: You define the topic, and the AI returns professional-grade content briefs.
  2. Generate Assets: Based on the brief, the system generates high-quality images in the correct orientation.
  3. Render: With a single command, you trigger the final rendering process.
  4. Polling/Retrieval: The API handles the heavy lifting, allowing you to monitor the progress and grab your final file once complete.

Advanced Customization: Using Your Own Assets

While the AI-generation capabilities are impressive, you aren’t locked into them. The Lowkey Viral skill supports custom asset uploads. If you have specific brand assets, photography, or custom design elements, you can upload them to the platform via a pre-signed URL and incorporate them into your video briefs. This allows you to combine the speed of AI generation with the unique aesthetic of your own brand.

Getting Started: Prerequisites

To start using the Lowkey Viral skill within OpenClaw, you will need a few essentials:

  • An Active Subscription: You must have a PRO or ULTIMATE plan from LowkeyViral.com.
  • API Key: Generate your unique key from the user dashboard.
  • Environment Variable: For security and ease of use, store your API key in an environment variable named LOWKEY_VIRAL_API_KEY.
