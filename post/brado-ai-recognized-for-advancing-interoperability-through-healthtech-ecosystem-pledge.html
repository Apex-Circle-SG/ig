What is the HealthTech Ecosystem Pledge?

The HealthTech Ecosystem Pledge is a collaborative initiative designed to foster open standards and seamless data exchange between healthcare providers, technology vendors, and insurance payers. By signing this pledge, companies like Brado AI commit to breaking down the barriers that prevent critical patient information from flowing securely between platforms. This initiative addresses the long-standing issue of fragmented health records, which often lead to diagnostic delays and fragmented treatment plans.
