The Science of the ‘Socialite’ Prompt

The guide simplifies the daunting task of prompt engineering into repeatable formulas. By breaking down the components, you can construct complex prompts that generate consistent results. Here are the three primary style formulas offered by the skill:

1. The Relaxed Chic (松弛感)

This is for the ‘effortless’ look. It focuses on soft natural lighting, a relaxed, candid-style pose, a pastel color palette, and a cozy environment like a high-end cafe or a luxury home office.

2. The Expensive/Noble Look (贵气感)

For a more formal, high-fashion editorial look. This utilizes golden accents, flawless, high-contrast makeup, and settings like hotel lobbies, focusing on jewelry and sophisticated textures.

3. The Refined Detail (精致感)

This style is about precision. It utilizes symmetrical composition, a focus on specific details (like a designer bag or silk fabric), and soft shadows to create a premium, high-quality photograph.

Technical Tips: Lighting and Posing

A photograph is only as good as its light. The OpenClaw skill provides specific, actionable keywords that you can plug into your prompts to change the atmosphere of an image instantly:

  • Golden Hour: Ideal for soft, warm, and romantic lighting.
  • Studio Glam: Use ‘butterfly lighting’ or ‘Rembrandt lighting’ to sculpt the subject’s face for that professional fashion editorial look.
  • Cinematic: Utilize ‘moody shadows’ and ‘film grain’ to give your image a storytelling quality.

Similarly, for posing, the guide recommends specific body language. For standing shots, it suggests the ‘S-curve’ or looking back over the shoulder. For sitting, it emphasizes elegant, crossed-leg poses or leaning on an armrest, which helps create a relaxed, natural connection between the subject and the viewer.
